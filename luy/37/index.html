37.Those drawn up in Ranks

  1. Nditsuba khubemaanga khulunyaali
  2. Nende khubalia bakaninjia amabii
  3. Nende khubalia basoomanga obwitsulisio
  4. Toto Nyasaye wenyu nimulala butswa
  5. Nyasaye Omulesi welikulu nende eshialo nende ebili hakari wabio. Ne ni Nyasaye Omulesi we ebukwe tsiosi
  6. Toto efwe khwarona likulu liahambi khutsing’ining’ini
  7. Khulwokhulinda okhurulana nende buli Shetani Omukhayi
  8. Balanyoola okhuwulila ebilonje bioluyali, ne baloondungwayo buli mumbeka
  9. Baloondungwa ne bali nende eshinyasio shilawa
  10. Halali ulia upusulanga eshindu eshititi , ne mana shimulondangakhwo eshikhupo shimesamesanga
  11. Barebekhwo mbu koo abo ni baloongwa okhuloongwa okhutinyu muno okhushila ebilonje bindi biakhwaloonga? Toto efwe khwabaloonga okhurulana khuliloba litilanga
  12. Owukanga nabo bakholanga omubayo
  13. Ne nibetsulisibwa shibetsulilanga tawe
  14. Ne nibalola eshimanyisio nibakhola omubayo
  15. Ne baboola mbu: “kano shinishiosi shiosi tawe halali ni obulosi butswa buli habulafu.”
  16. Mbunikhufwanikhumalileokhubaliloba nende ebikumba, kho toto khulilamusibwa
  17. Kata bapapa befu bakhale
  18. Boola mbu: “mmm! nanyu nimuli batutuyibwe .”
  19. Eyo oluyoka lulikhupwa lulala butswa, ni hawo niwo abalilola
  20. Baliboola mbu: “ abayiee, ino niyo inyanga yomurungo.”
  21. Ino niyo inyanga yeyindukho yamwakhayanga
  22. Bakhung’asie boosi bakhola amabii nende abakhasi babu nende balia boosi babalaamanga
  23. Nibakhaya okhulaama Nyasaye bachomie khunjila yomulilo kwa Jahannam
  24. Mana mubeemie. Totobo balarebwa
  25. “Mubeele murie? Shichila shina nimulakhoonyana?”
  26. Halali olwalelo balaloonda lilako
  27. Balihengana abeene khubeene nibarebana
  28. Baliboola mbu: “ toto enywe mwakhwitsilanga okhurula omukhono omulunji”
  29. Balibakalusia mbu: “Halali enywe abeene shimwali Abasuubili tawe.”
  30. “Efwe shikhwali nobunyali khwinywe tawe halali enywe abeene mwali abakori”
  31. Mana omuse kwa Nyasaye wefu Omulesi kumalile okhubura. Toto efwe khulapila butswa eshinyasio
  32. Khwabakosia eshichila mbu fwesi khwali abakori
  33. Toto abo inyanga eyo balisaanga halala mushinyasio
  34. Endio nilwakhulikholela Abamabii
  35. Olwa baboolelungwa mbu: “abulaho Nyasaye ulaamwa khubwatoto halali Nyasaye Mulala butswa- besuunganga
  36. Baboolanga mbu efwe khuleshesa Abanyasaye befu okhubeela Omukaasi wetsinyimbo wuno Omulalu
  37. Halali ye yetsa khubwatoto ne afuchilisania nende Abarumwa
  38. Toto enywe mulipila eshinyasio eshilulu
  39. Ne shimulirungwa tawe halali kalia akamwakholanga
  40. Halali Abasumba ba Nyasaye abalobole
  41. Abo nibo balinyoola omukabo omubiishe
  42. Ebiamo , nabo balihebwa oluyali
  43. Mumikunda chobwokholo
  44. Baliba khubisaala nibahengane
  45. Balibotokhanilibwa eshisanda shieshinywebwa shieyisebele
  46. Eshinywebwa eshilafu , eshimilimili khu ushinywa
  47. Shibula obubii , nohomba shishibameesia tawe
  48. Ne baliba nende Abakhasi betsimoni tsietsisoni tsindayi
  49. Abakhasi shinga amabuyu kaliindwa
  50. Baliinjila bachake , okhureebana abeene khubeene
  51. Aliboola omubooli mulala mubo: “toto esie ndali nende Omwitsa.”
  52. Yambolelanga mbu: “lano ewe ulimubafuchililanga?”
  53. ‘‘Mbu nikhufwa khwaba liloba nende ebikumba, mbu khulirungwa mana khwabalwa?’’
  54. Aliboola mbu: “ laano enywe mulola murie?”
  55. Mana alimuhenga kho alimulola hakari wa Jahannam
  56. Aliboola mbu: “wallahi, Nyasaye mulala wali ahambi okhungosia.”
  57. Tsikhayanga okhuba tsimbabaasi tsia Nyasaye wanje omulesi ndakhabeele mubalia abenjisibwamwo
  58. Koo! Efwe shikhulifwa tawe
  59. Halali okhufwa khwefu khwokhuranga, nafu shikhulinyoola eshinyasio tawe
  60. Toto khuno nikho okhubura okhukhongo
  61. Ne shinga kano, nikabakhole balia bakhola
  62. Koo okhubukanilwa endi shinokhulayi nohomba omusaala kwa Zaquum
  63. Toto efwe khwakukhola okhuba eshinyasio khubalia abamabii
  64. Toto oko ni omusaala kurulanga hakari wa Jahannamu
  65. Ebibuli bie biamo biakwo bili shinga omurwe kwa mashetani
  66. Toto abo balilia ebiamo ebo mana betsusie tsinda tsiabu
  67. Mana khu ako belesibwe amaatsi kayile karabachila
  68. Mana obwatoto amakalushilo kabu kaliba Jahannam
  69. Toto bo banyoola Basabwe nibakora
  70. Nabo nibakhola lwangu okhuloonda amaseenelo kabu
  71. Ne khubwatoto bamala okhukora imbeli wabu Abandu bakhale abaanji
  72. Ne toto efwe khwabarumila abekanyilisi
  73. Mana henga olole shingala indukho yabalia ebekanyilisibwa
  74. Halali Abasumba ba Nyasaye abalobole
  75. Ne toto Nuhu Yakhulaanga , ne efwe khuli abalayi po abafuchililanga
  76. Ne nikhumuhabina ye halala nende Abandu be okhurulana nende amasila amakhongo
  77. Mana nikhukhola olwibulo lulwe okhutoong’a
  78. Mana nikhulekha ifuma indayi khubetsa inyuma wuwe
  79. Omuleembe kube khu Nuhu mushialo shioosi
  80. Toto endio nilwakhurunganga abakholi bamalayi
  81. Toto ye ali Mubasumba befu Abasuubili
  82. Mana Abandi abo nikhubasishia mumatsi
  83. Ne toto Ibrahim yali mumukaanda kukwe
  84. Olwa yetsela Nyasaye wuwe Omulesi nende Omwoyo kukwe kwoosi
  85. Olwa yaboolela Papa wuwe nende Abandu bebe mbu: “nishina shiamulamaanga?”
  86. Eminyasaye chiobubeyi echilali Nyasaye nichiamwenyanga?”
  87. ‘‘Mupariranga shina Nyasaye Omulesi webilonje bioosi?”
  88. Mana nasitia tsimoni okhuhenga tsing’ining’ini
  89. Naboola mbu: “toto esie endwala.”
  90. Nabo nibamulekha nibamufutarira omukongo
  91. Mana nabwondela Eminyasaye chiabu. Nachiboolela mbu: “shichila shina nimulaliitsanga?’’
  92. ‘‘Mubeele murie amulalomalomanga tawe?’’
  93. Mana nachikalukhanila nachikhupa nomukhono omulunji
  94. Mana nibamwitsila lwangu lwangu
  95. Naboola mbu: “ enywe mulaamanga ebindu bia mwiraanyanga abeene?”
  96. Ne Nyasaye niye wabaloonga halala nende akamukholanga ako
  97. Nenibaboola mbu mumumbashile eshiumbashe mana mumusukune mumulilo
  98. Benya okhumukholela amabii halali nikhubakhola bo okhuba bahasi
  99. Ne naboola mbu : “ toto esie ewuya enzia ewa Nyasaye wanje Omulesi, ye alalunjisia”
  100. Ewe Nyasaye wanje Omulesi, mbelesie ulia uli mubakholi bamalayi
  101. Mana nikhumusubisia Omwana Omwihotselefu
  102. Olwa yoola eshichelo shiokhuchenda ninaye yamuboolela mbu: “ewe mwana wanje , toto esie endutsi mumaloro mbu ekhusinzanga, kho hengamwo olola orie?” naboola mbu: “ ewe papa wanje, khola shinga olomesibungwa witsa okhunyoola Nyasaye nachama nendi mubalia befwiila”
  103. Kho boosi babili olwa behotselesia , mana namukonelia obweni
  104. Nikhumulanga mbu : “ewe Ibrahim!”
  105. Omalileokhukholahabwenikhumaloro ako, toto endio nilwakhurunganga Abakholi bamalayi
  106. Toto kano ni amatemo kali habulafu
  107. Mana nikhumuhelesia eshisinzo shioluyali
  108. Mana nikhuleshela ifuma indayi khubetsa inyuma
  109. Omulembe kube khu Ibrahim
  110. Endio nilwakhurunganga Abakholi bamalayi
  111. Toto ye ali Mubandu befu basuubila
  112. Ne nikhumusuubisia okhwibulwa khwa Is’haaq, naye ni Omurumwa womubandu Abalayi
  113. Mana nikhumuhelesia tsimbabaasi ye nende Is’haaq. Ne mulwibulo lwabu nibarulamwo Abandu abalayi nende balia bakhola amabii khumioyo chiabu habulafu
  114. Netoto khwakholela obulayi Musa nende Harun
  115. Nikhumuhabina halala nende Abandu babu okhurulana nende obutinyu obukhongo
  116. Mana nikhubahabina nibaba nibo abashila
  117. Ne nikhuhelesia babili abo eshitabu shinoosinjia
  118. Mana nikhubalunjisia khunjila ingololofu
  119. Mana nikhubaleshela ifuma indayi khubalia betsa inyuma wabu
  120. Omulembe kube khu Musa nende Harun

  141. Mana nibasanjilana mumakhuwa, mana nikamukwiila
