18.The Cave

  1. Oluyali lwoosi nilwa Nyasaye oweshia eshitabukhumusumbawuwe, neshiyashikhola okhuba mashiama tawe
  2. Shiema obulayi, kho shikanyilisilie Abandu eshinyasio eshilulu shirulanga khuye, ne shihelesie amacheni amalayi balia basuubilanga abakholanga ebikhole ebilayi, mbu balinyoola omurungo omulayi
  3. Bekhalemwo tsinyanga tsiosi
  4. Ne shikanyilisie balia baboolanga mbu: “ Nyasaye alinende Omwana.”
  5. Abo babula obumanyi balio kata baasabwe, nilikhuwa likhongo muno lirulanga muminwa chiabu. Shibaboolanga halali obubeyi butswa
  6. Ne onyala okhwiyira ewe omwene, khulwa okhubabelelela ngala bali mbu shibasubilanga amakhuwa kano tawe
  7. Toto efwe khwakhola ebili khushialo bibe ebirone biashio, kho khubalole ni wina mubo webikhole ebilayi
  8. Ne toto nefwe khukholanga bioosi ebo bili hekulu walio okhuba liloba eliumu
  9. Nohomba oparanga mbu Abandu bo mulwanda, nende bakalia kahandikwa, mbu bali abamakana okhurulana nende ebimanyisio biefu
  10. Olwa Abasiani abo belushila mulwanda, mana nibaboola mbu: “Nyasaye wefu khuhelesie tsimbabaasi tsirulanga khwiwe nekhukasilie abulunjifu mulikhuwa liefu.”
  11. Mana nikhusuba amarwi kabu mulwanda omo khumika eminji
  12. Mana nikhubalamusia khokhumanye mbu nikuli hena khumikanda chibili chino okwa bala obulayi ebise biabamenyamwo
  13. Efwe khukhukanilanga amakhuwa kabu khubwatoto, toto abo ni ababukha abasuubila Nyasaye wabu, mana nikhubametela obulunjifu
  14. Mana nikhura amani mumioyo chiabu, olwabeema mana nibaboola mbu: “Nyasaye wefu ni Nyasaye welikulu nende eshialo,shikhulalaamakhwo Nyasaye wundi ulali Ye tawe. Toto awo khulaba khuboolele obubii obwaburirira eshichelo
  15. “Abandu befu bano, bakhola abanyasaye balali ye tawe. shichila shina nibalarerakhwo eshifune shili habulafu, kho ni omubii shina omukhongo okhushila ulia uchishilanga Nyasaye obubeyi
  16. “Ne olwamwali nimubawukhekhwo, nende bilia ebiabalaamanga habandu wa Nyasaye, mana mwilushila mulwanda, Nyasaye wenyu alabekulila tsimbabaasi tsitsie ne alabakholela amakhuwa kenyu okhuba amangu
  17. Ne alola eliuba olwalirulanga libura ehale mulwanda lwabu namukhono musatsa. Ne ulwa likwitsanga namukhono omukaata. Nabo nibali mukhombe mukari wo lwando. Ebo ni bilala khubimanyisio bia Nyasaye, ulwa owayalunjisia oyo niye omulunjifu, ne awaalekha okhukora, kho oyo shalamuhelesia omulindi umulunjisia tawe
  18. Ne opara mbu bali meso, nabo khane bakonele, nekhubakalukhaninjia namukhono mulunji, nende namukhono mukata. Ne imbwa yabu nilambisiyie ebilenje biayio mubwinjilililo, obalolanga wakhakalukhane lwangu okhubelukha, ne toto oburi bwakhakhwitsulemwo shichila bo
  19. Kho endio nilwakhwabalamusia kho bareebane abeene khubeene, naboola omubooli mubo mbu: “mwikhalemwo tsinyanga tsinga?” naboola mbu : “ khwikhalemwo inyanga ndala nohomba ebuleka weyinyanga.” Nibaboola mbu “ Nyasaye wenyu amanyile muno ebise bia mwekhalamwo. Kho mana murume mulala wenyu nende amamondo kenyu kano khushiro mana ahenje eshiakhulia shina eshilayimunoabarerere. Neakholeamakhuwa kano mumachesi ne alabarumbulakhwo khuyesiyesi tawe
  20. Shichila bo nibamanya hamuli balabalasa amachina, nohomba balabakalusia mumilukha chiabu, ne awo shimulibura kata hatiti tawe
  21. Na endio nilwakhwabarumbusia, khobanyoole okhumanya mbu indakano ya Nyasaye ni yatoto ne mbu inyanga yeyindukho ibula obukanakani tawe, olwa basolananga abeene khubeene shichila likhuwa liabu. Nyasaye yamanya obulayi. Balia bashila mulikhuwa liabu nibaboola mbu: “Toto efwe khulabombashila omusikiti.”
  22. (Abandi) baboolanga mbu: “Bali Abandu bataru wakhane wabu ni imbwa yabu” Ne (abandi) baboolanga mbu:” Bali Abandu barano ne wakhasasaba ni imbwa yabu,”(babolanga) khu khutemakho butswa okhuboola-akabakorerwe. Nebaboolanga mbu bali Abandu saba ne wamunane wabu ni imbwa yabu. Boola: ‘‘Nyasaye wanje ni umanyile mbu bali benga. Abulaho umanyile halali batiti butswa. Kho olayingana nabo tawe halali okhuyingana khwe hekulu butswa. Ne olareba amakhuwa kabu omundu yesi yesi tawe
  23. Ne olabolakhwo khuliosi liosi tawe mbu: ‘‘Nditsa okhukhola mutsuli.”
  24. Halali (ometekho) “ Inshallah(Nyasaye nachama) Ne yitsulila Nyasaye wuwo niwibiliile, ne boola mbu : “Nyasaye wanje alalunjisia injila ili ahambi muno nende obulunjifu okhushila buno.”
  25. Ne bamenya mulwanda mwabu omo emika tsimia tsitaru, nenibametakhwo tisa
  26. Boola “Nyasaye amanyile muno ibise biabekhalamwo” niye umanyile akefisile ako mwikulu nende khushialo. Okhulola khuli khurie nende okhuwulila khukhwe, nabo babula omulindi ulali ye, naye shatsokasinjiakhwo yesiyesi mubwami bubwe tawe
  27. Ne soma akawafimbulilwa okhurula mushitabu shia Nyasaye wuwo. Abulaho unyala okhukalukhasia amakhuwa keke. Nawe sholinyoola awilushila okhumwilukhaye akhaye okhwunyoola tawe
  28. Ne wisumilisie halala nende balia balaamanga Nyasaye wabu etsuli nende ingoloobe, nibakhaba obuchami bubwe. Ne olababusiamwo tsimoni tsitsio khulwokhwenya ebirone bio bulamu bwokhushialo tawe. Ne olaria balia abakhwalekha bakandala emioyo chiabu mana nibalonda obwikombi bwabu, mana amakhuwa kabu nikabura eshichelo
  29. Ne boola mbu: “ buno ni obulaamba okurula khu Nyasaye wenyu’’ kho ulia wenya asuubile ne owenya akhaye efwe khwarecheshela ababii omulilo, okwe tsikhwi tsiakwo tsilibabotokhana . Ne nibenya obukhonyi balikhonywa amatsi shinga shichuma shisalulusibwe shisaamba obweni, eshinywebwa eshibi shili shirie shino! Nende habandu ali abii arie hano
  30. Toto balia abasuubila mana nibakhola amalayi, efwe toto shikhukosinjia omurungo kwabalia bakholanga amalayi tawe
  31. Abo balinyoola emikunda chilaliwa tawe, echiburangamwo emialo- imbeli wachio, balironwamwo nende abifwalo biokhumakhono, ebie idhahabu, ne balifwala tsingubo tsia majani-etsia hariri inyangu nende hariri isiro. Balikhalamwo khu bitali ebiaronwa (khufono). Ni umurungo omulayi kuli kuriena kuno! Ne ni habandu alayi ali arie hano
  32. Ne bachelasilie, Abandu babili, mulala wabu khwamukholela emikunda chibili chetsizabibu, mana nikhubotokhania mitende, ne hakari nikhuraho emisala chindi chieyimele
  33. Emikunda chibili chino chioosi chietsulamwo ebiamo biachio ne shikhwalemiakhwo shiosi shiosi tawe. Ne mukarimwo nikhubusiamwo emialio
  34. Ne yali nende ebiano ebinji. Mana naboolela owashie nibakalusaninjia mbu: “Esie ekhushila khumwandu nende amani khulwa Abandu bendi ninabo.”
  35. Mana niyenjila mumukunda kukwe niyekholele obubii naboola mbu: “ shembara omukunda kuno nikulibiyakhwo tsinyanga tsiosi tawe.”
  36. “Ne shembara indukho nilibaho tawe, ne nindilikalusibwa khu Nyasaye wanje ndilinyoola amekhalo amalayi okhushila kano.”
  37. Owashie namuboolela nibakalusaninjia mbu “ Koo; Okhayanga ulia wakhuloonga khuliloba, mana khu indonyande ya amatsi kolwibulo, mana nakhukhola omundu omulaamba?”
  38. “Halali ye Nyasaye niye omulesi wanje ne shenzokasinjiakhwo shiosi shiosi nende Nyasaye wanje tawe.”
  39. Ne shingala winjile mumukunda kukwo oboolanga mbu: “biendi nabo bino Nyasaye niye unyenyeele, amani shikanyolekhanga halali khu Nyasaye,” ni undolanga mbu endi nende omwandu omutiti nende Abana abatiti okhushila ewe.”
  40. Awundi Nyasaye wanje alambelesia eshilayi okhushila omukunda kukwo, ne yaruma (mukunda oko) likulu okhurula mwikulu mana libe liloba likhaya- lirerekha
  41. “Nohomba amatsi kalio kakhame obule okhukanyoola.”
  42. Mana ebiamo bayie nibibotokhanwa, mana natong’a okhukalukhania amakhono keke, khulwa obukhalabani bwaye (omo) nabo (ebiamo) nibibula khumisala chiachio, mana naboola mbu: “Ehh! manyanga ekhaya okhutsokasia Nyasaye wanje nende shiosishiosi”
  43. Ne shiyali nende Abandu bokhumukhonya tawe niyakhamala okhukhaya Nyasaye, nohomba shiyali omwene unyanala okhwikhonya tawe
  44. Eyo obwami nibwa Nyasaye butswa owatoto, Naye niye omurunji omulayi, owokhurera amarulilo amalayi
  45. Ne bachelasilie obulamu bwokhushialo buli shinga amatsi kakhwishinjia okhurula mwikulu, mana nikesasia nende emimela chiomwiloba, mana emimela echo nichiba amasafu amomu, akakhalishe khalishe akomuyaka kukhuyunga eyi- nende eyi. Ne Nyasaye ali nende obunyali khubuli eshindu
  46. Omwandu nende Abana ni ebirone bio bulamu bwokhushialo. Ne ebikhole ebilayi bitong’anga nibo obulayi imbeli wa Nyasaye wuwo khumurungo nende okhusuubilila okhulayi
  47. Ne inyanga yakhulichesia tsingulu, ne olilola eshialo nishili eshiang’ang’u, ne khulibalamusia neshikhulilekha yesiyesi mubo tawe
  48. Ne baliemibwa imbeli wa Nyasaye wuwo olunyaali. Toto enywe mukhwitsile shingala khwabalonga olwambeli. Halali mwapara mbu shikhulibarereho ebise bino tawe
  49. Ne ebitabu bilirebwaho- ( imbeli wabu). olilola shinga ababii baliria shichila kalia kalimwo. Ne baliboola mbu: “Oh bayie, ebitabu bino bili birie, shibilekha lititi nohomba likhongo halali liandichilwe.” Ne balinyoola koosi kabakhola nikali hawo, ne Nyasaye wuwo shakholelanga yesiyesi obubii tawe
  50. Ne olwakhwaboolela Malaika mbu: “inamile Adam.” Mana nibenama halali Ibilis, ye yali mumukanda kwa majini, mana nafunaka lilako lia Nyasaye wuwe. Koo mumukhola ye nende olwibulo lulwe abetsa (benyu) habundu wa Nyasaye, nabo nibali abasuku benyu? Ni obubii buli burie bwababii bakalukhaninjia
  51. Shindabakhola okhuba abaloli olwa okhulongwa khwe likulu nende eshialo nohomba okhulongwa khwabu abeene, ne shindakhola abakosinjia okhuba abakhonyi tawe
  52. Ne inyanga yaliboola mbu: “Rerekhwo abamwasanjinjia ninaasi abamwachikhanga,” mana balibalanga mana shibalibafuchililila tawe, mana khulira hakari wabu eshikalilo
  53. Ne Ababii balilola omulilo, ne balimanya mbu okhula balenjilamwo, Ne mana shibalinyoola amelushilo tawe
  54. Ne toto khwaboolela Abandu Mukurani ino buli imbia yakhufwananishia. Halali Omundu yabura eshichelo okhushila buli eshindu mukhuyingana
  55. Ne shibulaho eshikayila Abandu okhusuubila olwa obulunjifu bwabetsela mana nibasaba eshileshelo khu Nyasaye wabu, halali kaboolele kalia akabakhale, nohomba eshinyasio shibetsela mapwoka
  56. Ne shikhurumanga Abarumwa halali babe abarusia amakhuwa amalayi nende 18. abekanyilisia. Ne balia bakhaya bayingananga khumakhuwa kalali katoto, mbu khobakhole obwatoto okhuba obubeyi. Ne bakholanga tsinyali tsianje nende kalia akabekanyilisibwa okhuba omubayo
  57. Ne ni wina omubii omukhongo okhushila ulia owitsulisibungwa tsinyaali tsia Nyasaye wuwe mana nalatsibukulila oluchendo mana niyebilila akemikhono chichie chiaranjilisia. (bilishinga) toto efwe khwara mumioyo chiabu ebifunikho kho bakhaye okhumanya, (bilishinga) efwe khwara mumarwi kabu obusiro. Ne nobalanga khubulunjifu shibakhachama okhulunjikha tawe
  58. NeNyasayewuwoniyeomunjiweshileshelo, Owetsimbabaasi, ababukulilanga khulwa kalia akamwakhola, toto yakhabanguyisiliye eshinyasio lwangu lwangu. Halali bali nende ebise ebia balalinyoola amelushilo kosikosi akokhwilukha tawe
  59. Ne tsingongo etsio khwatsisishia olwa tsiakhola obubii, nikhubarerarao ebise biokhusikha khwabu
  60. Ne olwa Musa yaboolela omusiani waye mbu: ‘‘Ndalatsililila okhutsia, okhula ndule etsinyanza tsibili tsibukananilanga nohomba enzilile emika nemika.’’
  61. Kho olwa boola etsinyanza tsibukananilanga nibebilila enyeni yabu, mana naye natsililila okhutsia imbeli munyanza
  62. Olwa boola imbeli yaboolela omusiani wuwe mbu: “ khwelesie eshiakhulia shiefu shieshitere, toto khunyoolele khuluchendo lwefu luno amachonye.”
  63. Naboola mbu: “ ulutsi, wakhuwulushile halia khushitulo? Owo niwo wandibilile- enyeni eyo. Ne abulaho owakhola ndebilila halali shetani mbu ndalayitsulila tawe, Mana natila injila munyanza okhutsia khwamakana
  64. Naboola mbu : “Alia niho wakhwenyanga” mana nibakalukha inyuma okhulonda injila yabu. (ilia ilia)
  65. Mana nibanyoola Omusumba khubasumba befu owakhwahelesia tsimbabaasi okhurula khwifwe, ne owakhwechesia amachesi okhurula khwifwe
  66. Musa namuboolela mbu: “Koo; Enyala ndakhulondakhwo unzechesiekhwo kobulunjifu kawechesibwa?”
  67. Naboola mbu: “Toto ewe shonyala okhwifwila okhuba halala ninasi tawe.”
  68. “Ne onyala orie okhwifwila kalia akokorerwe obulamba bwako?”
  69. Naboola mbu: “Nyasaye nachama, witsa ukhunyoola nendi omwisumilisi, ne shinditsa okhulobakhwo likhuwa lilio liosi liosi tawe.”
  70. Naboola mbu: “Mana niwunodakhwo, olandeeba liosi liosi tawe, okhula esie anjake okhuboolela.”
  71. Mana nibarula, olwa banina obwaro, nabufutula. Musa namureeba mbu: ‘‘Ofutula obwaro kho osishie abalimwo? Toto okholele likhuwa libii muno”
  72. Naboola mbu: “shekhuboolelekhwo mbu sholanyala okwifwila ninasi tawe?”
  73. Naboola mbu: “Olambukulila khukandibilile tawe, noho alasitoyelia amakhuwa kanje tawe.”
  74. Mana bosi nibarula halala, Nibanyoola Omusiani mulala mana namwira . Musa naboola: “Ahh wira Omundu eshikhaya ubula likosa, ulerere Omundu tawe? Toto okholele likhuwa libii muno!”
  75. Naboola mbu: “Koo; shekhuboolelekhwo mbu sholanyala ekhwifwila halala ninasi tawe?”
  76. Naboola mbu: “Nekhureeba liosi liosi khandi olakhola okhuba halala ninawe khandi tawe, shichila omalile okhunyoolakhwo eshifune.”
  77. Mana nibatsililila okhutsia olwa boolela Abandu bolukongo, nibareeba abeene olukongo olo eshiakhulia nibabeema okhubakholela obucheni, ne nibanyoolamwo- (mulukongo olo) inzu lisisi lienyanga okhukwa- mana nibaliemia. Musa naboola mbu: “noli uchama khwakhabukule omurungo (Khumilimo chino)”
  78. Naboolambu:“kunonikwo omwawukhano kwanje ninawe. Nditsa okhuboolela obwatoto bwakalia kolanyalile okhwisumilisia ninasi.”
  79. Kho eliaro liali liabamanani bakholanga munyanza, ne ndenyele okhulirakhwo isoni, shichila imbeli wabu Omwami yaliyo unuulanga buli eliaro lilayi
  80. “Ne umusiani ulia, abebusi bebe bali abasuubili, nikhuria mbu aletsa yabayila mubukori bwokhuloba nende okhukhaya,”
  81. “Kho mana nikhwenya Nyasaye wabu abakalukhanilie Omwana omulayi okhushila oyo, khubutswenufu, nende owuli ahambi muno khushisa
  82. Nalo lisisi, liali lia Abaana abafwilwa babili, mulitokho elo, ne hasi walio khwaliho nende libishibo liabu, ne samwana yali omundu omulayi, kho Nyasaye wuwo yenya mbu bakubuye mana berusilie abeene libishilo liabu, etso nitsimbabaasi tsirula khu Nyasaye wuwo. Ne shekholele kano khubunyali bwanje tawe . Obo nibo abwatoto bwa kalia akokhayilwe okhwifwila ninaasi
  83. Nebakhurebanga amakhuwa ka Dhul’karnain boola mbu;’ Nditsa okhubasomela malala khumakhuwa keke”
  84. Toto efwe khwamutinyia khushialo, ne nikhumuwa injila yokhunyoola buli eshindu
  85. Mana naloonda injila
  86. Okhuula shingala yoola ebukwe eliuba likwitsanga, yalilola nilikwa niliyeela khusebele, eya matoyi amanji. Awo nanyoola Abandu. Nikhuboola mbu: “Ewe Dhul’karnain; Bahelesie eshinyasio nohomba abakholele obulayi.”
  87. Naboola mbu: “ulia owakhola obubii khwitsa okhumuwa eshinyasio, mana alikalusibwa khu Nyasaye wuwe, naye alimuhelesia eshinyasio eshibii muno
  88. Ne ulia owasuubila mana nakhola ebikhole ebilayi, alanyoola omurungo omulayi, nafu khwitsa okhumuboolela amakhuwa kefu amangu
  89. Mana naloonda injila
  90. Okhula shingala yoola eliuba lirulilanga (mumbo) yalilola nilirulila khubandu abalakhwarereho eshishinjilo shiokhubekalila (ninalio)
  91. Ne shinga endio khwali nikhumanyile obulayi amakhuwa koosi kayali ninako
  92. Mana naloonda injila
  93. Okhula shingala yoola hakari wetsingulu tsibili, yanyoola inyuma wuwe Abandu balawulilanga amakhuwa tawe
  94. Nibaboola mbu: “ Ewe Dhil-Karnain; Toto Yaajuj nende Maajuj, banyasinjia eshialo, kho khukhwelesie omurungo ore eshikalilo hakari wefu ninabo?”
  95. Naboola mbu: “Kalia ka Nyasaye wanje yandinyisiliamwo ni amalayi muno, khomukhoonye nende amani ndalara hakari wenyu ninabo eshikalilo eshisiro.’’
  96. “Munderere ebitonye bieshichuma” okhula shingala yetsusia obwiyangu buli hakari wetsingulu tsibili; naboola mbu: ‘‘Bwaane; (omulilo)” okhula shingala yakhola eshichuma shilia omulilo, naboola mbu: ‘‘Munderere ishaba isalulushe enzushe khuno.”
  97. Mana shibanyala okhunina nohomba okhufutula tawe
  98. Naboola:”Tsino ni tsimbabaasi okhurula khu Nyasaye wanje. Ne olwa yiliula indakano ya Nyasaye wanje, alikufunakafunaka. Ne indakano ya Nyasaye wanje ni yahabwene
  99. Ne khulilekha abandi babu nibasutania sutania (mumulilo) Ne olwa olwika lulikhupwa. Awo khulibakhung’asia boosi halala
  100. Ne khulilolosia Jahanamu inyanga eyo imbeli wabakhayi malolosia (mang’alang’ala)
  101. Abetsimoni tsiabu tsiali tsiafunikhwa khumetsulilo kefu, neshibali nibanyala okhuwulila tawe
  102. Koo, Balia abakhaya baparanga mbu okhukhola Abasumba banje abalindi babu, habandu wanje (khulabakhonya shiosi shiosi)? Toto efwe khwarechekha Jahannam abakhayi ibe abaliishila
  103. Boola: “Koo ebechesiekhwo balia bebikhole biabu biabiiya?”
  104. Ni balia bobukhalabani bwabu bwakora eshikhaya khubulamu bwokhushialo, nabo baparanga mbu bakholanga ebikhole ebilayi
  105. Abo nibo bakhaya ebimanyisio bia Nyasaye wabu nende okhubukanana ninaye. Kho ebikhole biabu nibikwa akhaya, ne shikhulibemelia obusiro inyanga yeyindukho tawe
  106. Kho Jahanam niko omurungo kwabu shichila bakhaya mana nibakholela omubayo ebimanyisio bianje nende Abarumwa banje
  107. Netoto balia abasuubila mana nibakhola amalayi emeshelo kabu kaliba Firdaws
  108. Balimenyamwo tsinyanga tsiosi omo, shibalienya okhurulamwo tawe
  109. Boola mbu: “Inyanza yibetsanga mbu nibo obwino (bwokhuhandika) amakhuwa ka Nyasaye wanje, inyanza yakhomele amakhuwa ka Nyasaye wanje nikashili okhuwa, kata khureranga etsindi shinga etso
  110. Boola mbu: “Toto esie endisa Omundu shinga enywe efimbulilungwa mbu Nyasaye wenyu ni Nyasaye mulala , kho ulia wikomba okhubukanana nende Nyasaye wuwe akhole ebikhole ebilayi ne alatsokasiakhwo shiosi shiosi khu milukha cha Nyasaye wuwe tawe
