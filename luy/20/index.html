20.Taa-Haa

  1. Ta’ha
  2. Shikhwakhwishilia Ikurani mbu khonyoole obutinyu tawe
  3. Halali ibe ametsulisio khu ulia uwihotselesia
  4. Ameshelo karulanga khu ulia owalonga liloba nende likulu lili hekulu
  5. Niye Nyasaye Owetsimbabaasi, Owaleng’aana khushisala shishie shioluyali
  6. Nebibie ebili mwikulu nende khushialo nende bili hakari wabio, nende ebili hasi weliloba
  7. Ne nolomaloma hekulu,(nohomba yahasi) toto ye yamanya akefisa nende akali mubwifisi muno
  8. Nyasaye abulaho ulaamungwa khubwatoto halali Ye ne ali nende ameera amalayi
  9. Ne koo, wawulilakhwo olukano lwa Musa
  10. Olwa yalola omulilo, mana naboolela Abandu bebe mbu: “Linde mbeli, esie toto endutsi omulilo, ndakhaba endio ndabarerera eshichenga shiomulilo oko, nohomba enyala okhunyoola khumulilo oko obulunjifu.”
  11. Olwa yakuulakhwo, yalaangwa: “ Ewe Musa;”
  12. Toto esie nesie Nyasaye wuwo omulesi, kho rusia ebilaro bibio, toto ewe oli mulitwoma lioluyali elia Tuwa
  13. Ne esie ekhuloboole, kho wulila, akofimbulilungwa
  14. Toto esie nesie Nyasaye abulaho ulaamungwa khubwatoto owulali esie, kho unaame ne olumilile khuswala khulwokhunzitsulila
  15. “Toto indukho itsia okhuula, ndenya okhuyifisa katong’ane, kho buli omundu arungwe khukalia akayakholanga.”
  16. Kho balakhwikalila okhusuubila, balia abalasuubilanga abaloondonga butswa obwikombi bwe emioyo chiabu abeene oletsa wasikha tawe
  17. Ni shina esho shili mumukhono kukwo omulunji ewe Musa
  18. Naboola Mbu: “Ino ni indabushi yanje yandisililangakhwo, ne eyikwisilinjia amasafu ke ebiayo bianje, ne khandi echikhonyelanga khumilimo chindi.”
  19. Nyasaye naboola mbu: “Isukune hasi ewe Musa”
  20. Mana nachisukuna, nikalukhana inzukha imoolanga tsimbilo
  21. Nyasaye naboola mbu; “Ibukule ne olaria tawe, bulano buri khulayikalusia shingala ibeele.”
  22. Ne ore omukhono kukwo mukwaya, kularula nikuli omulafu tswe, kubula obubii bwosi bwosi, shino ni eshimanyisio shindi
  23. Kho khukhwechesie ebimanyisio biefu ebikhongo
  24. Tsia khu Firaun, Toto ye yabura eshichelo
  25. Naboola mbu: “ Ewe Nyasaye wanje mbangulile eshilifu shianje
  26. Ne okhole amakhuwa kanje okhuba eshichelo. amaangu
  27. Ne okasie okhuboyokana khwolulimi lwanje
  28. Banyoole akhuwulila amakhuwa kanje
  29. Ne ukholele omukhoonyi okhurula mubandu banje
  30. “Omusiani wefu Haruni.”
  31. Undemo amani okhumwikhonyela ye
  32. Ne umure halala khumilimo chianje chino
  33. Kho khunyoole okhukwitsoomia muno
  34. Ne khukhurumbule mubunji
  35. Toto ewe okhulolanga
  36. (Nyasaye) Naboola mbu: Toto ofuchililwe amasayo koko ewe Musa
  37. Ne toto khwakhukholela obulayi khandi olundi
  38. Olwa khwafimbulila mama wuwo kalia akakhwamufimbulila
  39. Mbu mure mwisanduku, mana olire mumwalo, mana omwalo kulamusukuna khulukuku, anyoole okhubukulwa nende omusuku wanje khandi nende omusuku wuwe, ne ekhureremwo obuyanzi okhurula khwisie, ne mbu olelwe mutsimoni tsianje
  40. Olwa omukhana wenyu yatsia naboola mbu: “ koo abechesiekhwo Omundu unyala okhumulela? Mana nikhukhukalusia khu mama wuwo kho tsimoni tsisie tsisangale ne kho alabeelela tawe. Ne wera Omundu mana khwakhuhabina okhurula mushibeela, mana nikhukhutema amatemo amanji, ne niwikhala emika eminji halala nende Abandu ba Madyan mana khandi witsile shingala walachilwa ewe Musa
  41. Ne ekhuloboole khulwa esie omwene
  42. Tsia ewe nende omusiani wenyu halala nende ebifune bianje, ne mulachonya okhunzitsulila tawe
  43. Tsie khu Firauni, toto ye yaburirira
  44. Mumuboolele amakhuwa amalayi, yakhaaba ario yatila amachelo nohomba yaria
  45. Nibaboola mbu: “ewe Nyasaye wefu; efwe toto khuritsanga aletsa yakhuburirirakho eshichelo nohomba yakhufurirakhwo.”
  46. (Nyasaye) Naboola mbu; “Mularia tawe, toto endi halala ninanyu, ewulilanga ne endolanga.”
  47. “ Tsie mana mumuboolele mbu: “Toto efwe khuli Abarumwa ba Nyasaye wuwo. Kho khuleshele Abaana ba Israil barule halala ninafu ne alabasandia tawe. Toto khukhwitsile nende ebimanyisio birula khu Nyasaye wuwo. Ne omulembe kulaba khubalia baloondanga obulunjifu.”
  48. Toto efwe khurererwe obufimbuli, mbu eshinyasio shilanyoola balia abayingasinjia nende okhufutarira omukongo
  49. Naboola mbu: “Nyasaye wenyu ni wina, ewe Musa? ”
  50. Naboola mbu: “Nyasaye wefu ni ulia uwahelesia buli eshindu ifwani yashio mana nashilunjisia.”
  51. Naboola mbu: “Ne Babura balia baliba barie?”
  52. Naboola mbu: “Obumanyi bwako buli khu Nyasaye wanje mushitabu, Nyasaye wanje shakoranga nohomba shiyebililanga tawe.”
  53. “Ulia wabakholela liloba okhuba obulili, nabarereramwo injila, mana niyeshia amatsi okhurula hekulu, ni khu ako nikhumesiliakhwo emimela chikabukhaane.”
  54. Lie ne mulisie ebiayo bienyu, toto mukano khuli nende ebimanyisio khubandu bobwongo
  55. Okhurula khulino(liloba)khwabaloonga ne mulio khulibakalusiamwo, ne mulio khulibarusiamwo khandi . (mube abalamu)
  56. Ne toto khwamwechesia ebimanyisio biefu biosi, halali yayingana nakhaya
  57. Naboola mbu: “witsile okhurusia khushialo shiefu khubulosi bubwo, ewe Musa?”
  58. “Mana efwe khwitsa okhurerera ubulosi shinga obo. Kho khure hakari wefu ebise, biokhubukanana, ebia khulafunaka efwe nohomba ewe tawe. Abe habundu halayi
  59. (Musa) Naboola mbu: “Ebise bienyu ni inyanga yoburone. Ne Abandu bakhung’asibwe itsuli.”
  60. Mana Firauni nakalukha mana nakasia emiare chiche, mana niyetsa
  61. Musa nababoolela mbu: “Abaane enywee; mulachishila Nyasaye obubeyi tawe, yakhetsa yabamala nende eshinyasio , ne toto yamala okhubiyilwa ulia uchikhanga.’’
  62. Kho mana nibasolana khulwa makhuwa ako abeene khubeene, mana nibasanjilana mubwifisi
  63. Nibaboola mbu:“Toto Abandu bano ni abalosi, benya okhubarusia mushialo shienyu khu bulosi bwabu, ne benya okhurusiaho enjila yenyu yabulamu indayi po.”
  64. “Kho lano busie halala ebifwanga bienyu biosi mana mwitsie nimuli khulunyaali, netoto Nyanga yino ulashila niye witsa okhuba hekulu.’’
  65. Nibaboola mbu: “ewe Musa, ewe newe ularanjilila okhusukuna nohomba efwe khuranjilile?”
  66. Naboola mbu: “Enywe ranjilile.” Mana emikoye chiabu nende tsidabushi tsiabu nitsilolokhana khuye mubulosi bwabu mbutsichendanga
  67. Mana Musa niyenjilwamwo oburi mumwoyo kukwe
  68. Nikhuboola mbu: “Olaria tawe, toto ewe newe witsa okhushila,”
  69. “Kho sukuna shili mumukhono kukwo omusatsa, shitsa okhumila biabakasiyie ebo. Toto bakasiye butswa ebifwanga biobulosi, ne Omulosi shaburangakhwo wosiwosi watsila tawe.”
  70. Mana abalosi boosi nibakwa hasi okhwinama nibaboola mbu: “Khusuubile khu Nyasaye wa Haruni nende Musa.”
  71. (Firauni) Naboola mbu: “Ehh; mumusuubile neshili okhubafuchilila. Toto ye niye Omukhongo wenyu owabechesia obulosi. Kholano nditsa okhubateta emikhono chienyu nende amakulu kenyu buchikha ne nditsa okhubabaamba khumisala chietsitende, ne mulamanyakhwo mbu niwina mwifwe omululu weshinyasio shiokhutsililila
  72. Nibaboola mbu: “shikhulalobola ewe tawe okhushila ebimanyisio bili habulafu bikhwitsile. Khwitsuba khu ulia wakhuloonga (shikhulakhulobola tawe) kho kholakhwo shiosi shiosi shia wenya okhukhola. Ewe Onyala okhurusia shibura shilondokhana nende obulamu bwokhushialo khuno butswa.”
  73. “Toto efwe khusuubile Nyasaye wefu kho akhuleshele abionono biefu nende okokhunichilisiye kano mubulosi. Ne Nyasaye nomulayi khandi niwokhubaho tsinyanga tsiosi.’’
  74. Toto ulia ulitsa khu Nyasaye wuwe, nali mbu niwamabii, toto alinyoola omulilo kwa Jahanamu, shalifwamwo tawe nohomba shalimenyamamwo (obulamu obulayi tawe)
  75. Ne ulia ulimwitsila nali omuusubili owakhola ebikhole ebilayi, abo nibo balinyoola ebisaala biehekulu
  76. Emikunda chilaliwa tawe, chiburangamwo hasi wachio emialo, balimenyamwo tsinyanga tsiosi. Ne oko niko omurungo kwa buli ulia wetswenula
  77. Ne toto khwafimbulila Musa, mbu: “Orule nende Abasumba banje, ne obakholele Injila yokhulukongo inyumu munyanza, olaria okhunyoolwa nohomba alaria shiosi shiosi tawe.’’
  78. Firauni nabalondakhwo halala nende omukanda kukwe kwabalwani, mana nikabanyoola munyanza omo akabanyoola
  79. Mana Firaun nakosia Abandu baye, ne shiyabalunjisia tawe
  80. Enywe Abana ba Israil, toto efwe khwabahabina okhurulana nende abasuku benyu, mana nikhumulaka mbu mwitse ebuleka womukhono musatsa weshikulu shia Tur, mana nikhubeshilia manna nende salwa
  81. Lie mubilayi bilia ebiakhubakabile, ne mularuuma olwakho khu ako tawe, liruma lianje liakhetsa liabatila. Ne ulia weliruma lianje linyoola alaba amalile okhusikha
  82. Ne toto esie endi wokhuleshela muno khu ulia owusaba eshileshelo mana nasuubila mana nakhola amalayi mana natila obulunjifu
  83. Nenishina shiakhuyungubasia, nolekha Abandu bobo ewe Musa
  84. Naboola mbu: ‘‘Bali inyuma wanje banondangakhwo, ne ekholele lwangu okhwitsila Nyasaye wanje, kho onyoole okhumba obuchami (bwo)
  85. (Nyasaye) Naboola mbu: “khuhelesiye Abandu bao amatemo ewe niwakharula, ne Omusamaria abakosiyie.”
  86. Mana Musa nakalukha khu Bandu bebe nasinyishe nabeelele. Naboola mbu: “Enywe abandu banje Nyasaye wanje shiyabalakakhwo indakano indayi? Koo ebise ebo bimurambiyile ebiamalakano ako nohomba mwenyele mbu lirumwa okhurula khu Nyasaye wenyu libeshele, khulwendio mufunache indakano yanje
  87. Nibaboola mbu: “efwe shikhufunache indakano yiyio khukhwenya khwefu tawe, halali efwe khwatukhwa emisiko chiebirone biabandu, mana nikhuchisukuna (mumulilo) (nichisalulukha nichikasia eshifwanani shia Nyasaye.) Na endisa nilwa Omusamaria yarusia amachelo.’’
  88. Mana nabakasiliamwo eshimosi, shilikho eshikhuli shirusinjia oluyoka. Mana nibaboola mbu; “Uno niye Nyasaye wenyu nende Nyasaye wa Musa, halali (Musa) yebilila.”
  89. Koo, shibalolakhwo mbu (eshimosi esho) sishibakalusinjia likhuwa tawe, nohomba sishiakhabakhola shiosi shiosi tawe nohomba okhubakhoonya
  90. Ne Haruni yababoolela khale awo mbu; “Enywe Abandu banje, toto enywe mutemelwe sa nende kano. Ne toto Nyasaye wenyu ni ulia owetsimbabaasi. Kho munoonde ne muwulile amakhuwa kanje.”
  91. Nibaboola mbu; “Shikhulalekha okhushilaama tawe okhula shingala Musa alachelela.’’
  92. (Olwa Musa yachelela) Yaboola mbu: “Ewe Haruni nishina shikhukayile (ukhunoonda) shingala obalutsi nibakorere?”
  93. ‘‘Shiwunoondakho tawe? nohomba olobele likhuwa lianje?’’
  94. (Haruni) Naboola mbu; “Ewe Omwana wa mama wanje, olakhwesa tsindefu tawe nohomba omurwe kwanje tawe. Toto esie ndaria mbu oletsa waboola mbu esaakulile Abana ba Israil.’’
  95. (Musa) Naboola; “Ewe Omusamaria; obele orie ukhula okhukhola kano?”
  96. Naboola; “Endutsi kabalalolanga tawe shakwalala. mana nendila ebuleka inditi yo mulukha kwo Murumwa. Mana nesukuna. Ne endio nilwa omwoyo kwanje kumboolele.’’
  97. (Musa) naboola mbu; “mana rula tsia,! ne toto kalaba khwiwe mubulamu bwao bwokhushialo okhuboola mbu: “olandilakhwo tawe’” ne toto oli nende indakano eyilalifunakwa tawe. Ne khahengekhwo Nyasaye wuwo owotsilile okhumulaama, toto khwitsa okhumusamba mana khulamwibisia munyanza akoreremwo
  98. Toto Nyasaye wenyu ni Allah (Nyasaye), ulia uli mbu abulaho ulaamungwa khubwatoto halali Ye. Yaswania buli eshindu obumanyi
  99. Ne endio nilwakhukhukanilanga khumakhuwa kali imbeli wuwo, Ne toto khwakhuhelesia ametsulilo okhurula khwifwe
  100. Balia betsa okhuba ehale ninako, toto abo balichinga inyanga yeyindukho emisiko
  101. Balimenyamwo tsinyanga tsiosi, ne ni obubii buli burie khumundu inyanga yeyindukho okhuchinga emisiko echo
  102. Inyanga yolwika lulikhupwa, ne khulikhug’asia aboononi inyanga eyo tsimoni tsiabu nitsikalukhane (khuburi)
  103. Balimonyaniamwo abeene khubeene, nebaliboola mbu: “shimwekhala khushialo halali tsinyanga ekhumi butswa”
  104. Efwe khumanyile muno akabaliboola, Olwa abalayi mubo baliboola mbu: “Enywe shimwekhalamwo halali inyanga ndala yonyene.”
  105. Nebakhurebanga amakhuwa ketsingulu, boola mbu: “Nyasaye wanje alitsisia masia.”
  106. Ne alitsilekha shinga eshikuri
  107. Sholilolamwo eshikholomokho noho eshitulo tawe
  108. Inyanga eyo balilonda khumulanji owubula ubokori, ne emioyo chilihotselesia khu Nyasaye Omunji wetsimbabaasi, ne sholiwulila halali tsimbindo tsiebilenje
  109. Inyanga eyo khubulaho ukhusabilwa khwosi khwosi halali khu ulia wachamilwa nende Nyasaye, umunji wetsimbabaasi mana afuchilile likhuwa lilie
  110. Yamanya akali imbeli nende akali Inyuma wabu. Nabo shibanyala okhumumanya obulayi tawe
  111. Ne obweni bulitutuyila Omulamu Ulafwitsanga tawe, Netoto baliba nibabiyilibwe boosi balia bachinga obubii
  112. Ne ulia ukhola amalayi niyasuubila kho shaliria okhukholelwa obubii nohomba okhwimwa eshishie tawe
  113. Ne endio nilwakhweshia Ikurani mulwarabu, ne nikhwinoosiamwo emekanyilisio ketsimbia tsiosi khobanyoole okhuria, nende okhwilinda ne ibarerere ametsulisio
  114. Ne toto Nyasaye niwoluyali owehekulu, Omwami watoto, ne olayuyukhanila Ikurani (okhusoma) obufimbuli bwayio nibushili okhuwa. Ne boola mbu: “Nyasaye wanje umeete obumanyi.”
  115. Ne toto khwalaka Adam khale indache, mana niyebilila, ne shikhwalola khuye lichomo likhongo tawe
  116. Ne olwa khwaboolela Abamalaika mbu mwinamile Adam, boosi nibamwinamila halali ibilisi, yaloba
  117. Ne nikhuboola mbu: “Ewe Adam, toto uno ni omusuku wuwo nende womukhasi wuwo, kho alabayinia mumukunda tawe mwaba khubutinyu.”
  118. Toto ewe sholawulilamwo inzala tawe nohomba okhuba eshiekenyi tawe
  119. Ne toto ewe sholiwulilamwo obulo tawe nohomba oluya
  120. Mana shetani namubeyiabeyiamwo, namuboolela mbu: “ewe Adam okhumanyiekhwo omusala kwokhumenya tsinyanga tsiosi nende obwami bulawa tawe
  121. Mana nibalia khumusaala oko, mana eshiekenyi shiabu nishibaloleshela. Nibachaka okhwifimba amasafu komumukunda omo. Mana Adam nakosela Nyasaye wuwe nokora injila
  122. Mana Nyasaye wuwe namwawula namuhelesia eshileshelo ne namulunjisia
  123. Naboola mbu: “yishemwo mwesi, nimuli abasuku abeene khubeene, ne obulunjifu okhurula khwisie nibuboolela, ulia ulalonda obulunjifu bwanje shalikora nohomba shalisanda tawe.”
  124. Ne ulia ura emukongo obwitsulisio bwanje alaba nende eshimenyo shiobusandi ne inyanga yeyindukho alilamusibwa nali omuwofu
  125. Aliboola mbu: “Nyasaye wanje khulwashina niwunamusia nendi omuwofu ne ndali nendolanga
  126. Namuboolela mbu: “Endio nilwabiakhwitsila ebimanyisio biefu nawe nobibilila kho kata wesi inyanga yino wibililwe.”
  127. Ne endio nilwakhurunganga ulia waburirisia eshichelo nende ulasuubilanga ebimanyisio bia Nyasaye wuwe tawe, ne eshinyasio shiomwikulu ne eshilulu muno eshilawa tawe
  128. Shibalolangakhwo ni emikanda chinga chia khwasishia chiachendanga mutsingo tsiabu, toto mukano khulimwo nende ebimanyisio khubandu bamachesi
  129. Nelikhayanga okhuba likhuwa liaranjilila okhurula khu Nyasaye wuwo, nende ebise biarebwaho, toto eshinyasio shiakhoolele (shiokhusishia aboononi abo)
  130. Kho wisumilisie khukababoolanga, ne witsoomie oluyali lwa Nyasaye wuwo eliuba nilishili okhurula nende nilishili okhukwa, nende ebise bieshilo biosi omwitsomie nende hakari weshitere wakhaba orio khowanyoola akakhuchamisia
  131. Ne olakhondiola tsimoni tsitsio khubwokholo bwa khwakholela abaandi mubo tawe oburone bwobulamu bwokhushialo kho khunyoole okhubatemamwo, Ne omukabo kwa Nyasaye wuwo ni omulayi kulaliwa tawe
  132. Ne lomesia Abandu bobo okhulumilila okhulaama Iswala ne otinyilisie omwene hawo, shikhukhusabanga omukabo kwosi kwosi tawe, nefwe khukhukabilanga ne indukho indayi ni khu ulia uli noburi (khu Nyasaye)
  133. Ne baboola mbu: “shichila shina nalakhwitsila nende eshimanyisio okhurula khu Nyasaye wuwe?” Koo shibiabetselakhwo ebimanyisio ebia habulafu ebili mubitabu biakhale
  134. Ne khubasishinjia khushinyasio Omurumwa oyo nashili okhwitsa, bakhaboolele mbu: “Ewe Nyasaye wefu, shichila shina nolakhurerera Omurumwa, khwaloonda ebimanyisio bibio, nikhushili okhututuyibwa endi, nende okhunyoola tsisoni
  135. Boola mbu: “Buli mulala alindanga (omurungo kukwe) kho nakhalinde, muletsa okhumanya ahambi hano mbu ni babu wina abali khunjila ya habwene ne babu wina balunjikha.”
