29.The Spider

  1. ALif Lam Mym
  2. Koo, Abandu baparanga mbu balalekhwa butswa shichila baboolanga mbu “khwasuubila” Kho shibatemwa tawe
  3. Toto khwatema balia abaali imbeli wabu, ne toto Nyasaye alabamanya balia abahabwene ne toto alamanya ababeeyi
  4. Koo, balia bakholanga amabii baparanga mbu balakhushila efwe. Okhubusia okhubii muno khwababusinjia
  5. Ulia wenya okhubukanana nende Nyasaye, Toto ebise bia Nyasaye bitsa okhuula, naye niye omuwulili muno, omumanyi po
  6. Ne ulia ukhalabana toto yekhalabanila omwene, Toto Nyasaye nomuyinda khubiloonje bioosi
  7. Ne balia basuubila mana nibakhola amalayi, toto khulibaleshela amabii kabu ne khulibarunga amalayi kabu kabakholanga
  8. Ne khwebaalila Omundu mbu akhole amalayi khubeebusi beebe, ne nibakhunichilisia okhusanjia nende shiosishiosi eshiobula nashio obumanyi, alabawulilatawe, ewanjenehoamulikalukha ne ndilibaboolela akamwakholanga
  9. Ne balia basuubila mana nibakhola amalayi toto khulibenjisia mubandu abalayi
  10. Ne mubandu baliho ababoolanga mbu khusuubile Nyasaye, ne olwa basinyisibwa khunjila ya Nyasaye, bamalanga bakhola amabii ka Abandu okhuba shinga eshinyasio shia Nyasaye. Ne obuhabini okhurula khu Nyasaye wuwo nibuula ni baboola mbu; “Toto efwe khubeele halala ninanyu” Koo! Nyasaye shamanyile akali mumioyo chiabandu tawe
  11. Ne toto Nyasaye alara habulafu balia basuubila ne alababuula abeshikhabili
  12. Ne balia abakhaya baboolela balia abasuubila mbu; “Loonde khunjila yefu, efwe nafu khulachinga tsimbii tsienyu” Ne shibali banyala okhuchinga khutsimbi tsiabu shiosi shiosi tawe. Toto bo ni ababeeyi
  13. Netoto balichinga emisiko chiabu, nende emisiko chindi halala nende echiabu, Ne toto balirebwa inyanga yeyindukho khukalia akabachikhanga
  14. Netoto khwaruma Nuhu khubandu bebe, mana namenya nabo emika iyelefu okhuyiniakhwo emika amakhumi karano, mana amatsi nikababukula nabo nibali aboononi
  15. Mana nikhumuhabina nende Abandu beisafina, ne nikhukhola shibe ishimanyisio khubandu
  16. Ne Ibrahim olwa yaboolela Abandu bebe mbu; “Mulaame Nyasaye nemumurie, ako ni amalayi khwinywe ni muba nimwakhamanya”
  17. “Toto enywe mulaamanga ebifwanani nimulekha Nyasaye ne mukasinjia obubeyi, toto balia abamulamanga abalali Nyasaye abo shibanyala okhumuhelesia enywe omukabo tawe, kho khaabe omukabo kwenyu ewa Nyasaye ne mumulaame ne mumukhupile orio. Ewuwe ni amulikalusibwa
  18. Ne nimuyingasia, bamala okhuyingasia Abandu baliho imbeli wenyu. Ne shikali khu Murumwa tawe halali okhusia khwa habulafu
  19. Koo, shibalolangakhwo shinga Nyasaye achachinjia ebiloonje, mana khandi alibikalusia. Toto ako khu Nyasaye ni amaangu
  20. Boola; “Chende khushialo mana mulolekhwo shingala yachachia okhuloonga, mana Nyasaye niye uliloonga ifwani yokhumalilikha. Toto Nyasaye ni Omunyali wabuli eshindu
  21. Ahelesinjia eshinyasio owayenya ne ahelesinjia tsimbabaasi owayenya. Ne ni ewuwe ni wamulikalusibwa
  22. Ne enywe shimunyala okhumushila (Nyasaye) khushialo nohomba mwikulu tawe, nanyu mubula omulindi nohomba omukhoonyi owulali Nyasaye tawe
  23. Nebaliabakhayaebimanyisiobia Nyasaye nende okhubukanana ninaye, abo nibo baliwesiakhwo omwoyo khutsimbabaasi tsianje. Nabo nibo abalinyoola eshinyasio eshilulu
  24. Mana amakalusio kabandu be shikali halali okhuboola mbu: “mumwire nohomba mumusaambe mumulilo” mana Nyasaye namuhonia okhurulana nomulilo, Toto mu ako khulimwo nende eshimanyisio khubandu basuubilanga
  25. Ne naboola mbu; “Toto enywe mwakhola ebifwanani okhuba Nyasaye, ne mwachamana abeene khubulamu bwokhushialo, halali inyanga yeyindukho muliikaanana abeene khubeene, ne mulitsubana ne eshimenyo shienyu shiliba mumulilo, ne shimulinyoola wokhubahabina tawe
  26. Mana Lut namusuubila, ne (Ibraheem) naboola mbu; “Toto esie ewuuya(khulwa) Nyasaye wanje toto ye niye Owamaani Owamachesi muno
  27. Mana nikhumwelesia Ishaq nende Yaqub, mana nikhukhola khulwibulo lulwe oburumwa, nende eshitabu, mana nikhumuhelesia omurungo kukwe khushialo. Netoto ye mwikulu aliba mubandu abalayi
  28. Ne Lut, olwayaboolela Abandu bebe mbu; “Toto enywe mukholanga eshifwoolo, eshia Omundu yesiyesi alabaranjililakhwo okhukhola tawe khushialo
  29. “Koo enywe mutsiilanga abasatsa, ne mulindanga Abandu khunjila, ne mukholanga amabii mumikhung’ano chienyu?” Kho mana amakalusio ka Abandu bebe shikali halali okhuboola mbu; “khurerere eshinyasio shia Nyasaye toto noli mubalia ababoolanga aka habwene”
  30. Naboola mbu; “Nyasaye wanje habine okhurulana nende Abandu abonooni.”
  31. Ne Abarumwa befu(Malaika) olwa betsela Ibraheem, nende amakhuwa amalayi, baboola mbu; “Toto efwe khwitsa okhusishia Abandu belitokho lino, toto Abandu balio ni abonooni muno.”
  32. (Ibraheem) Naboola mbu; “Toto Lut alimwo” nenibaboola mbu; “Efwe khumanyile balia balimwo, khwitsa okhumuhabina ye nende beyinzu yiyie halali omukhasi wuwe, owuli mubalia batong’a inyuma.”
  33. Ne olwa Abarumwa befu boolela Lut, yababeelelela muno, ne omwoyo kukwe nikulola obusiro muno khulwabo, mana nibaboola mbu; “olaria nohomba olabeelela tawe, toto efwe khwitsa okhuhabina ewe nende Abandu bobo halali omukhasi wuwo owuli mubalia batong’a inyuma
  34. “Toto efwe khwitsa okhwishia khubandu bolukongo luno eshinyasio okhurula mwikulu shichila okhuloba khwabu”
  35. Netoto khwalekhamwo eshimanyisio shiahabulafu khubandu baparanga
  36. Ne Madiani khwaruma Omusiani wabu mbu Shueibu, naye naboola mbu; “Enywe Abandu banje, mulaame Nyasaye ne murie inyanga yeyindukho, ne mulachenda khushialo nimukhola obwonooni tawe”
  37. Mana nibamuyingasia mana omurengo kweshialo nikubeera mama nibubashiela mutsinzu tsiabu nibalaambile mafumama
  38. Nende Babu A’di nende Thamudi (khwabasishia) Ne mushimenyo shiabu khubaloleshelanga (okhusishibwa khwabu) Ne shetani yabaronela ebikhole biabu, mana nabekalila injila (yamalayi) nabo bali nibalolanga
  39. Nende Karuni nende Firauni nende Haman, Netoto Musa yabeetsela nende obimanyisio ebiahabulafu mana nibelola khushialo, ne shibanyala okhumushila tawe
  40. Kho buli shiamulala wabu khwamutila nende eshinyasio khulwe tsimbii tsitsie, abaandi mubo khwabarerera imbumbeyeka yetsimbale, ne abaandi mubo baliwo barusibwa emioyo khuluyoka, ne abaandi mubo baliwo bakhwakhola liloba okhubamila, ne abaandi mubo baliwo basishibwa munyanza, ne Nyasaye shiyabakholela obubii tawe, halali bo abeene nibo bekholela obubii
  41. Balia abakhola abanyasaye bandi abalali Nyasaye tawe balishinga olububi lukasilinjia inzu, ne toto inzu indeshele muno okhushila etsindi ni inzu yolububi. Abayiee bamanyangakhwo
  42. Toto bwene Nyasaye yamanya ebiabalaamanga ebilali ye, nibalekha Nyasaye, naye niye Owaamani, Owamachesi muno
  43. Ne okho nikhwo okhuchelaasia khwakhuchelaasilinjia Abandu, ne shibaparanga (okhuchelasia okho) tawe halali abamanyi
  44. Nyasaye yaloonga likulu nende eshialo khubwatoto, toto mu ako khulimwo nende eshimanyisio khubasuubili
  45. Soma akawafimbulilwa mushitabu ne Olumilile okhulaama, toto okhulaama khwikalilanga eshifwolo nende tsimbi, ne obwitsulilo bwa Nyasaye nibwo obukhongo muno, Ne Nyasaye amanyile muno akamukholanga
  46. Ne mulayingana nende Abandu beshitabu tawe halali khukalia amalayi, okhuyiniakhwo khubalia bakhola obubii mubo, Neboole mbu; “Khusuubile khukalia keshibwa khwifwe, nende keshibwa khwinywe, ne Nyasaye wefu nende Nyasaye wenyu ni mulala, nafu khuli bokhwihotselesia khuye.”
  47. Ne endio nilwa khwakhwishilia eshitabu, nebalia abakhwahelesia eshitabu basuubilanga mushio, ne baliho mubano balia basuubilangamwo, ne shibakhayanga ebimanyisio biefu tawe halali Abandu abakhayi (balia benya okhufisa obwatoto)
  48. Ne shiwali niwasomakhwo eshitabu shiosi shiosi tawe, obwatoto nibushiili okhurula, nohomba shiwashihandikakhwo nomulunji kwo tawe, bakhabeele neshibeyibeyi balia benyanga okhukalukhania obwatoto
  49. Halali tsino nitsinyaali tsili habulafu mubilifu bia balia bahebwa obumanyi, Ne shibakhayanga tsinyaali tsiefu tawe halali aboononi
  50. Ne baboola mbu “shichila shina naleshilibwa amangalimwa okhurula khu Nyasaye wuwe?” Boola mbu; “Amangalimwa kali khu Nyasaye yenyene butswa, nesie endi butswa omwikanyilisi owahabulafu.”
  51. Koo shikabayelasa mbu efwe khwakhwishilia eshitabu basoomelungwa, toto mu ako khulimwo nende tsimbabaasi nende ametsulisio khubandu basuubilanga
  52. Boola mbu; “Nyasaye ayela okhuba omuloli hakari wanje ninanyu, amanyile kali mwikulu nende mushialo, ne balia bafuchililanga obushiamu ne nibakhaya Nyasaye, abo nibo abalalinyoola obukhala.”
  53. Ne bakhuyungubashinjia mbu eshinyasio shiuule lwangu, ne bikhaya okhuba ebise bimanyikhane, eshinyasio shiakhaboolele, netoto shilibetsela mapwoka nabo nibalamanyile tawe
  54. Bakhuyungubasinjia khushinyasio, ne kali mbu toto Jahanam yabotokhanilibwa abakhayi
  55. Inyanga yeshinyasio shilibafunikha okhurula hekulu wabu nende hasi webileenje biabu, mana aliboola mbu; “pile kalia akamwakholanga”
  56. Enywe Abasumba banje basuubila, toto eshialo shianje neshibangufu, kho munaame esie butswa
  57. Buli omwoyo kulipila lifwa, mana ewefu niwamulikalusibwa
  58. Ne balia basuubila mana nibakhola ebikhole ebilayi, khulibekhasia mwikulu mutsinzu etsiburanga hasi watsio emialo, balimenyamwo tsinyaanga tsiosi, ne Omurungo omulayi kuli kurie kwabakholi
  59. Balia abesumilisia mana besikanga Nyasaye wabu butswa
  60. Ne nitsisolo tsinga atsilakhanyala okhuchinga omukabo kwatsio, ne Nyasaye atsikabilanga nende enywe mwesi, naye niye amuwulili muno omumanyi po
  61. Nenobareeba mbu; “Niwina waloonga 29. likulu nende eshialo, mana nakhola eliuba nende omwesi okhuwulila? Toto balaboola mbu; “Nyasaye” Kho niyena amukalukhasibungwa
  62. Nyasaye abambulilanga omukabo owayenya ne amufumbila owayenya, toto Nyasaye ni Omumanyi wabuli eshindu
  63. Ne nobareeba mbu; “Niwina wishinjia amatsi okhurula mwikulu, mana alamusie liloba nilimalileenje okhufwa?” Toto balaboola mbu; “Ni Nyasaye” Boola mbu; “Oluyali lwoosi luli khu Nyasaye” Halali abaanji mubo shibamanyanga tawe
  64. Ne obulamu bwokhushialo buno shini eshindu tawe halali amakhuwa kabulamwo oluchendo nende omubayo. Ne inzu yo mwikulu nibo obulamu bwatoto, abayie bamanyangakhwo
  65. Ne nibanina muliaro (Munyanza) balaamanga Nyasaye mubutswenufu, Ne niyakhamala okhubahabina nibakhoola khulukongo, nibachaka okhumutsokaasia
  66. Khobanyoole okhubula okhupa orio khukakhubahelesia mbu khobekhoye, halali ahambi hano balamanya
  67. Koo shibalolangakhwo mbu efwe khwakhola eshialo shiabu okhuba shioluyali nende omulembe, ne Abandu bayuulungwa mumbeka mwabu. Koo musuubilanga obushiamu ne tsimbabaasi tsia Nyasaye nitso tsamukhayanga
  68. Ne niwina omubii muno oyo, okhushila ulia uchishila Nyasaye obubeyi, nohomba ukhaya obwatoto nibumalile okhumuulila? Koo Jahanam shinishioshakhabe eshimenyo shabakhayi
  69. Ne balia bakhalabana okhubeela efwe, toto abo khulabalunjisia khutsinjila tsiefu, netoto Nyasaye ali halala nende bakholanga amalayi
